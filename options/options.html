URLhaus threat intelligence

URLhaus (by abuse.ch) is CookieSpy's strongest malware-domain signal. As of 2024 it requires a free Auth-Key. Without one, scoring still runs on the keyless DNS checks alone — but it catches far less.

Get a key in about two minutes: sign in at auth.abuse.ch with Google/GitHub/X/LinkedIn, then create an Auth-Key under the "Optional" section and paste it below.

Verifying the block UI

To see the auto-block and timed-release UI without hunting for a real malicious site, visit testsafebrowsing.appspot.com. CookieSpy treats that host as a built-in self-test entry and scores it 100, so it will appear blocked in the popup with the full allow menu.
